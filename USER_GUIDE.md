## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

```
Click "Sign Up" → Fill in your details → Select your semester → Click "Sign Up"
```

**What you need:**
- Username (unique identifier)
- Email (login credential)
- Password (secure it!)

- Use a memorable username

### Step 2: Log In

```
Click "Login" → Enter email and password → Click "Login"
```

Your session will be saved automatically!

## 📚 Questions & Answers

### Asking a Question

**Where:** Questions Section → "Ask New Question" button

**Steps:**
1. Enter a clear, concise **title**
2. Provide a detailed **description**
3. Select your **semester** (1-8)
4. Optionally add a **subject** (Math, Physics, etc.)
5. Click "Post Question"

**Tips for Good Questions:**
- ✅ "How to solve quadratic equations?"
- ✅ "What is the difference between GDP and GNP?"
- ❌ "Help me" (too vague)
- ❌ "URGENT ANSWER NEEDED" (no urgency needed)

**Best Practices:**
- Search before asking (avoid duplicates)
- Provide context and details
- Include what you've already tried
- Mention specific semester/subject

### Answering Questions

**Where:** Click on any question → "Post Answer" button

**Steps:**
1. Read the question carefully
2. Provide a **detailed, helpful answer**
3. Explain your reasoning
4. Click "Post Answer"

**Tips for Good Answers:**
- ✅ Provide step-by-step explanations
- ✅ Include relevant examples
- ✅ Correct grammar and spelling
- ✅ Be respectful and helpful

**Earn Points By:**
- Providing accurate answers
- Getting marked as correct by asker
- Default: 50 points per correct answer

### Searching Questions

Use the search bar to find questions by title, description, or subject. Results update as you type.

### Bookmarking Questions

Click the bookmark icon on any question to save it for later. Access your bookmarked questions from your profile.

### Voting on Answers

Upvote or downvote answers to highlight quality content. Click again to toggle/remove your vote.

### Deleting Your Own Questions

As the question author, you can delete your question with a single click. This also removes all associated answers, votes, and bookmarks.

### Question Lifecycle

```
Question Posted → Visible to all → Users answer → Asker marks correct → Points awarded
```

## 🔐 Account Management

### Password Reset

Forgot your password? No problem:
1. Click "Login" → "Forgot Password?" link
2. Enter your registered email address
3. A reset token will be shown (in development) or sent to your email
4. Use the token to reset your password securely
5. Log in with your new password

### Staying Secure

**Password Tips:**
- ✅ Use strong passwords (8+ characters)
- ✅ Mix letters, numbers, and symbols
- ✅ Don't share your password
- ✅ Don't reuse passwords from other sites

**Account Tips:**
- ✅ Use a valid email
- ✅ Log out on shared computers
- ✅ Keep your browser updated
- ✅ Enable security features

### Admin Panel

Administrators have access to the admin panel at:
```
http://localhost:5000/admin.html
```
Default credentials:
- **Email**: `admin@college.com`
- **Password**: `admin123`

**Admin Features:**
- Dashboard with platform statistics
- User management (view, edit points, delete)
- Question moderation (view, delete)
- Answer moderation (view, delete)
- Shop management (CRUD for items)
- Redemption history tracking

### Logging Out

**Steps:**
1. Click your profile button (top right)
2. Click "Logout"
3. Session ends, must login again to participate

**After Logout:**
- Can still view questions
- Cannot ask or answer
- Cannot redeem points

### Session Management

- Your login is saved locally in your browser
- Clear browser data to force logout
- Session persists even after browser close

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Can't Login
- ✅ Check email spelling
- ✅ Check password (case-sensitive)
- ✅ Ensure caps lock is off
- ✅ Try creating new account
- ✅ Clear browser cookies

### Can't Post Questions
- ✅ Must be logged in
- ✅ Fill all required fields
- ✅ Check your internet connection
- ✅ Try refreshing the page

### Can't See My Points
- ✅ Navigate to Rewards Shop
- ✅ Check that you're logged in
- ✅ Refresh the page
- ✅ Clear browser cache

### Redemption Issues
- ✅ Ensure you have enough points
- ✅ Item must be in stock
- ✅ Confirm redemption carefully
- ✅ Check your purchase history

### General Issues
- 🔧 Refresh page (F5)
- 🔧 Hard refresh (Ctrl+F5)
- 🔧 Clear browser cache
- 🔧 Try different browser
- 🔧 Check internet connection
